Network & Cyber Security

DESCRIPTION
 BrainTech Network & Cyber Security Championship is an Azure Skynet Solutions Pvt. Ltd. initiative that focuses on guiding students in their quest for a prime level of technical education. It offers high caliber training programs while enrolling participants under 2 levels of evaluation process. Under this stratagem, participants are assessed on theoretical & practical grounds & so are titled as Championship Holders. A scholarship worth Rs. 1 Lac is awarded at the Grand Finale of this program.
Registration fee is Rs.900/-
WORKSHOP CONTENT
 This program involves participants from a series of technical programs & events under the supervision of Azure Skynet Solutions Pvt. Ltd. which runs during the course of an academic year at allocated zonal centers (Engineering & Polytechnic Institutions) across the country. An event under this program includes a workshop on latest technologies, be it Cisco Networking,Ethical Hacking, Cyber Forensics, Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Android, etc. Post workshop, the participants appear for the first level of examination under which three Zonal Winners are selected. The winners are awarded a Certificate of Excellence qualifying them for the final round of the Championship called Grand Finale. In the final round, zonal winners are engaged in a time bound examination schedule laid out by our technical team. During the course of events, sessions by distinguished people in various fields are organized to allow participants have an exposure on the environment, challenges & opportunities they will seek in their pursuit for a thriving career.
